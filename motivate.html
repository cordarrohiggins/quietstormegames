Motivate

My first dive into game development with GameMaker.

About This Game

Motivate is a top-down shooter that puts you in control of a pint-size, gun-wielding wizard on a mission to defeat the enemies of the land. Blast through handcrafted arenas and face off against a variety of cunning foes and epic bosses. With tight controls, a driving chiptune soundtrack, and secrets hidden in every corner, Motivate turns every level into a test of reflexes and strategy.
