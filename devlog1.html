Devlog #1: Building the Website

June 18, 2025

Today marks the first step in bringing Quiet Storme Games online. I chose a clean, responsive template and set up our GitHub Pages repository, then customized the layout to match our studio’s branding. It was great to see the homepage come together in just a few hours.

After configuring the navigation, I created dedicated pages for our games, the devlog index, and the about section. I also implemented a contact form to let visitors reach out—testing it end-to-end to ensure messages will arrive correctly.

Next, I refined the visual hierarchy, adjusted typography, and added placeholder content so each section feels cohesive. The site now loads quickly on mobile and desktop, and I’m happy with how it scales at different screen sizes.

Up next in the devlog series: I’ll dive into integrating our first game prototype, share screenshots from early builds, and discuss lessons learned during development. Stay tuned!
