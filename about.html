About Quiet Storme Games

Quiet Storme Games is a one-person passion project born from my love of the magic that games can spark in people. I’m not a big studio - just a solo creator who stayed up too late writing code because I believe interactive experiences can change lives the way the greatest games changed mine.

Every design choice comes from a personal place: I want players to feel curiosity, hope, and connection. Whether it’s a small puzzle or a quiet moment of reflection, my goal is to craft moments that resonate long after the screen goes dark.

While I may wear every hat - programmer, artist, writer - I’m fueled by a single mission: to share stories and mechanics that remind us why we fell in love with games in the first place. Each prototype and level is another step toward something meaningful.

This site is the hub for that journey. Thank you for stopping by, and I hope you’ll join me as Quiet Storme Games grows - one heartfelt project at a time.
